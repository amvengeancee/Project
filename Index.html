What Is The Rolex Motors




       Rolex Motors, established in 2010 and located in the heart of Chennai, India, represents the pinnacle of luxury automotive excellence, specializing in the sale of exotic hypercars. As India’s only authorized importer of rare, high-end automobiles, Rolex Motors offers an exclusive collection of the world’s most prestigious and high-performance vehicles, including limited-edition hypercars that are sought after by enthusiasts worldwide. The state-of-the-art showroom features brands renowned for their exceptional design, cutting-edge engineering, and unmatched performance, providing a luxury experience that is meticulously curated for discerning enthusiasts. Rolex Motors is dedicated to offering its clients not just the finest vehicles, but a gateway to the extraordinary lifestyle that accompanies these incredible machines, making it a true icon in the luxury automotive market in India.The state-of-the-art showroom features brands renowned for their exceptional design, cutting-edge engineering, and unmatched performance, providing a luxury experience that is meticulously curated for discerning enthusiasts.

Not Only Selling




       At Rolex Motors, we pride ourselves on offering comprehensive services that cater to the specific needs of supercar and hypercar owners. Beyond our collection of rare and limited-edition vehicles, we provide personalized solutions that include customization, maintenance, and spare parts sourcing. Our customization services offer bespoke enhancements, allowing customers to modify their vehicles to reflect their individual tastes and elevate their driving experience. Furthermore, our expert team is well-versed in sourcing genuine parts to ensure seamless maintenance and longevity of each luxury vehicle. All maintenance and repair services are conducted with manufacturer-certified expertise, ensuring the utmost care and precision are taken in handling these sophisticated machines. Whether it is for routine maintenance or specialized services, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of care for our clients' prized automobiles.

            Our Story

                   The story of Rolex Motors began with a shared passion for luxury cars, nurtured by childhood friends Venkatesan Kannaiya (Venkat) and Deepak in Chennai, India, during the 1980s.



                   They grew older, their fascination with supercars only intensified. In 2008, Venkat's attendance at a luxury car expo in Geneva sparked a vision to bring the hypercar experience to India.



                   By their dream, Venkat and Deepak meticulously planned Rolex Motors over the next two years. They forged relationships with esteemed hypercar manufacturers and ensured their showroom would meet rigorous global standards.



                   In 2010, Rolex Motors opened its doors as India's pioneering hypercar dealership in Chennai. The luxurious facility, paired with personalized service, swiftly attracted high-net-worth individuals and automotive enthusiasts. The dealership's success was swift and remarkable.
